About the BMOS Program

The Bachelor of Management and Organizational Studies (BMOS) program at Western University offers an interdisciplinary approach to business education, combining core business disciplines with a broader social science perspective. The program prepares students for the dynamic global management environment through rigorous coursework and practical application.

Program Highlights:
  • Honours Specialization: Intensive program focusing on Finance with advanced coursework and research opportunities
  • Interdisciplinary Approach: Combines business management with organizational studies and social sciences
